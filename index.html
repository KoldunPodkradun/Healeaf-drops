What is CBD oil?

Your endocannabinoid system (ECS) regulates everything from relaxation, to eating, sleeping, inflammation and even cognitive function. In a nutshell, your ECS is responside for making sure your entire body is working optimally. There are two types of cannabinoid receptors found throughout your body (CBI and CM), but they are most abundant in your brain and immune system. CBD is an abbreviation for cannabidiol, a prominent naturally-occurring cannabinoid component found in cannabis that comprises up to 40% of the plant and helps to regulate your ECS. CBD is by far the most studied natural cannabinoid, especially when it comes to medical uses addressing issues such as insomnia and anxiety. According to many researchers, CBD may be the single most important cannabinoid ever discovered.
